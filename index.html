Nomoskar! 🙏

I am Bappa Ghosh, a postdoctoral researcher at the University of North Dakota, USA, in Prof. Motoki Takaku's research group. Currently, I am working on chromatin 3D structure and dynamics to understand cancer genome organization. I have completed my doctoral studies on non-equilibrium polymer dynamics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Pune, India. I have worked on multiple problems related to the behaviour of polymer chains under force through Langevin dynamics simulation and theory in Dr. Srabanti Chaudhury's group.
I am broadly curious about computers, chemistry and chromatin. This website is meant to share my activities on related topics.
