I am Bappa Ghosh, a postdoctoral researcher at the University of North Dakota, USA, with Prof. Jerome Delhommelle at MSNEP group. Currently, I am working on non-equilibrium statical thermodynamics of active matter systems to compute entropy production through compression algorithms, simulations and machine learning.
I have completed my doctoral studies on non-equilibrium polymer dynamics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Pune, India. I have worked on multiple problems related to the behaviour of polymer chains under force through Langevin dynamics simulation and theory in Dr. Srabanti Chaudhury's group. I have also worked on atomistic simulations of crystal growth of organic molecules and gas hydrate systems
I am broadly curious about computers and chemistry. This website is meant to share my activities on related topics.
